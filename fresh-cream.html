Fresh Cream

Our Fresh Cream is rich, smooth, and naturally extracted from high-quality milk. It enhances the taste of desserts, curries, and beverages while adding richness and texture.

A Commitment to Quality:

We focus on purity and richness, ensuring our fresh cream is clean, natural, and consistent in texture and taste.

Procedure:

Fresh milk is processed to separate cream using controlled methods. The cream is then pasteurized, cooled, and packed under hygienic conditions to maintain freshness and quality.
